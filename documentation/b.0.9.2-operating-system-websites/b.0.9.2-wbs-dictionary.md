\## WBS Dictionary for oS3 B.0.9.2 Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) Documentation Website&#x20;

\| WBS Element | Description |

\| --- | --- |

\| \*\*1. Strategic Definition\*\* | Defines the project's goals, objectives, and scope, ensuring alignment with business objectives and user needs. |

\| WBS 1.1 - Define Project Goals and Objectives | Outline SMART goals and objectives, involving stakeholders to ensure a clear direction. |

\| WBS 1.2 - Market Research and Analysis | Study target market, competitors, and user needs to identify unique features and value propositions. |

\| WBS 1.3 - Establish Project Scope and Boundaries | Determine scope, exclusions, and constraints. Develop a scope statement to define the project's problem, objectives, deliverables, and success criteria. |

\| WBS 1.4 - Identify and Engage Stakeholders | Create a stakeholder register, map their roles and responsibilities, and brief them on the project. |

\| \*\*2. Preparation and Briefing\*\* | Assembles the project team, defines requirements, and develops a detailed project plan. |

\| WBS 2.1 - Assemble and Brief Project Team | Bring together subject matter experts, designers, developers, and project managers. Conduct a kickoff meeting to share project scope and goals. |

\| WBS 2.2 - Define Project Requirements | Identify functional and non-functional requirements, user stories, and acceptance criteria. Create a product requirements document. |

\| WBS 2.3 - Develop Project Plan and Schedule | Create a comprehensive project plan with tasks, timelines, milestones, dependencies, and resource allocation. Identify risks and develop a risk management plan. |

\| WBS 3.1 - Create Website Architecture and Navigation | Design information architecture, sitemap, and navigation flow, ensuring they align with user needs. |

\| WBS 3.2 - Develop Visual Design Concepts | Explore visual concepts, branding, and style guides. Create mood boards and style tiles. |

\| WBS 3.4 - Review and Refine Concepts | Hold design reviews, gather feedback, and refine website concept, navigation, and content outlines. |

\| \*\*4. Spatial Coordination\*\* | Finalizes the website design, creates wireframes and prototypes, and conducts usability testing. |

\| WBS 4.1 - Finalize Website Design | Based on feedback, finalize design, ensuring it meets requirements and user expectations. Create high-fidelity prototypes. |

\| WBS 4.2 - Create Wireframes and Prototypes | Develop detailed wireframes and interactive prototypes to showcase functionality and user experience. |

\| WBS 4.3 - Conduct Usability Testing | Perform usability testing with users to gather feedback and identify issues. Analyze results and make improvements. |

\| WBS 4.4 - Integrate Feedback and Refine | Incorporate feedback from testing and reviews, ensuring accessibility and usability standards are met. |

\| \*\*5. Technical Design\*\* | Involves technical specifications, setting up the development environment, and implementing website functionality. |

\| WBS 5.1 - Develop Technical Specifications | Create detailed technical specs, including database design, programming languages, and integrations. Ensure security and performance requirements are met. |

\| WBS 5.2 - Set Up Development Environment | Establish development tools, frameworks, and platforms. Ensure version control and testing environments. |

\| WBS 5.3 - Implement Website Functionality | Develop core functionality, following technical specs. Implement interactive elements and dynamic features. |

\| WBS 5.4 - Conduct Internal Testing and Debugging | Perform internal testing to identify and address bugs, errors, and performance issues. Ensure cross-browser and device compatibility. |

\| \*\*6. Manufacturing and Construction\*\* | Populates content, finalizes the website, and prepares for launch. |

\| WBS 6.1 - Content Population and Review | Populate website with finalized content, ensuring accuracy, readability, and SEO optimization. Conduct content reviews. |

\| WBS 6.2 - Finalize Website and Launch Preparation | Complete final design touches, performance optimizations, and SEO. Prepare launch plan and marketing strategy. |

\| WBS 6.3 - Develop Launch Plan and Marketing | Create a launch timeline, marketing activities, and communication strategies. Prepare promotional materials. |

\| WBS 6.4 - Train Stakeholders and Users | Provide training sessions or tutorials for stakeholders and users to familiarize them with the website. |

\| \*\*7. Handover\*\* | Focuses on final testing, sign-off, and website launch, ensuring a smooth transition. |

\| WBS 7.1 - Final Testing, Quality Assurance, and Sign-Off | Conduct comprehensive final testing, including user acceptance testing. Obtain stakeholder sign-off and approval. |

\| WBS 7.2 - Website Launch and Go-Live | Execute the launch plan, making the website live and accessible. Monitor the launch process and address immediate issues. |

\| WBS 7.3 - Post-Launch Support and Maintenance | Provide ongoing support post-launch. Address user feedback and conduct regular updates and enhancements. |

\| \*\*8. Use\*\* | Monitors website performance, maintains content, and continuously improves the website based on user feedback. |

\| WBS 8.1 - Monitoring, Maintenance, and Updates | Regularly monitor performance, conduct maintenance, and address technical issues. Implement content updates and improvements. |

\| WBS 8.2 - User Feedback Loop and Continuous Improvement | Establish a user feedback mechanism to gather and act upon feedback. Identify opportunities for improvement and enhance the website. |

\| WBS 8.3 - Regular Website Reviews and Revisions | Conduct periodic website reviews to ensure relevance and alignment with user needs. Plan major revisions or redesigns as needed. |
